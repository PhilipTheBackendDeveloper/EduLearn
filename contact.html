Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us and our services

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer support line for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a virtual meeting with an education advisor?

Yes, you can schedule a virtual meeting with one of our education advisors. Simply fill out the contact form and mention that you'd like to schedule a virtual consultation. Our team will reach out to arrange a convenient time.

How can I get technical support for course access issues?

For technical support, please email support@edulearn.com with details about the issue you're experiencing. Include screenshots if possible. Our technical team will assist you as quickly as possible. You can also use the live chat feature during business hours for immediate help.

Do you offer group discounts for schools or organizations?

Yes, we offer special pricing for schools, districts, and educational organizations. Please contact our sales team at partnerships@edulearn.com to discuss your specific needs and receive information about our group rates and institutional licenses.

How can I provide feedback about a course or instructor?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback about courses or instructors by using the feedback form within your course dashboard. Alternatively, you can email feedback@edulearn.com with your comments. All feedback is reviewed by our quality assurance team.
